Kay's Salmon Wellington

Description

When my wife and I got married, she was not a good cook. This was not her fault of course, as her family monopolized the cooking in a single member of the household. A shame. Thankfully, Kay's a natural. This salmon wellington is the proof.

Ingredients

  • Filet of Salmon. We prefer sockeye.
  • Creamed Spinach
  • WetMozzarella cheese
  • Puff pastry
  • Egg wash: One egg beat into a bowl plus whatever seasonings you desire

Steps

  1. Thaw out the puff pastry
  2. Skin the salmon
  3. Cut the mozzarella and the creamed spinich in 1'' slices
  4. Unroll the puff pastry, place salmon in the middle, and place the mozzarella and spinach on top of the salmon.
  5. Pull the sides of the puff pastry up around the salmon and toppings, and pinch the dough closed.
  6. Brush the entire pastry with egg wash.
  7. Add whatever extra seasonings you want here. Kay like various combinations of chili powder, garlic, black pepper, and/or salt.
