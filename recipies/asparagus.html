Proscuitto-wrapped Asparagus

Description

A delicious snack or main course which is enhanced by the use of an air fryer. In the air fryer, the proscuitto crisps nicely, and the asparagus retains its fresh nutrients without adding extra unhealthy grease. All in all a great dish for any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • Asparagus, with the butts cut off
  • Proscuitto. We like the cheap non-Parma Aldi brand.
  • Cracked Black Pepper
  • Lemon
  • Hollandaise Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 350 for 5 minutes. (Always preheat!)
  2. While preheating, wrap the asparagus in proscuitto, and brush or spray with oil. Cover with cracked black pepper.
  3. Arrange wrapped asparagus in fry basket and cook for 5 minutes, remove and shake the fry basket, and then another 3 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with hollandaise and a lemon slice.
